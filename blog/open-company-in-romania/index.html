Why open a company in Romania

Romania offers EU market access, skilled workforce, competitive operating costs and a growing business ecosystem.

Where obligations start after incorporation

  • accounting records;
  • tax calendar;
  • VAT analysis;
  • payroll setup;
  • banking and document flow;
  • financial reporting.

How the process works

  1. choose the legal structure;
  2. register the company;
  3. set up accounting;
  4. analyze VAT obligations;
  5. prepare payroll if hiring;
  6. maintain tax compliance.

How to verify the accounting partner

Check credentials, experience with foreign investors and public proof of professional qualifications.
