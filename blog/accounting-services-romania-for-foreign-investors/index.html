Why accounting matters for foreign investors

Romanian accounting and tax rules affect incorporation, payroll, VAT, financial reporting and communication with authorities.

A local accounting partner reduces compliance risk and helps management focus on business growth.

Where foreign investors face difficulties

  • company setup and accounting onboarding;
  • VAT registration and reporting;
  • payroll for Romanian employees;
  • ANAF communication;
  • management reporting;
  • cross-border transactions.

How accounting support works

  1. review the company structure;
  2. set up accounting processes;
  3. define tax calendar;
  4. manage documents and reporting;
  5. handle payroll and VAT;
  6. provide management-ready reports.
